How myquantstrategy works

Whether you're buying a proven strategy or selling one you built, here's exactly how everything works.

For traders

Buy a strategy and start trading

No experience required. Every strategy is mechanical — the signals tell you exactly what to do. No guessing, no discretion, no emotional decisions.

1

Browse the marketplace

Every strategy shows verified metrics — total P&L, win rate, profit factor, max drawdown, and annual return. All numbers are independently verified. You also see Monte Carlo simulation results showing the probability of profit across thousands of scenarios.

2

Start your 30-day free trial

Pick any strategy and get full access for 30 days. No credit card required. The strategy loads on your TradingView chart as a protected indicator — you'll see entry signals, stop loss lines, take profit targets, and all filters automatically.

3

Follow the signals

When a signal fires on your chart, you take the trade. That's your entire job. No analysis, no chart reading, no second-guessing. The strategy tells you when to enter, where your stop is, and where your target is. 15-30 minutes a day.

4

Subscribe or cancel

If you love it, your subscription starts after the trial at the price the seller set. If not, just cancel — no charge, no hassle. You can subscribe to multiple strategies or cancel anytime.

For strategy creators

Sell your strategy and earn recurring revenue

You built something that works. Now let it earn for you while your code stays completely protected.

1

Submit your strategy

Fill out the seller application with your strategy details and backtest results. We review every submission — we only list strategies that meet our quality standards.

2

We verify everything

We independently verify your backtest results, run Monte Carlo simulations, and check for curve-fitting or unrealistic assumptions. If it passes, your strategy gets a full listing page with all verified metrics.

3

Publish as protected indicator

You publish your Pine Script strategy as a protected indicator on TradingView. Subscribers get added to your access list — they see signals but never see your code. If they cancel, they're removed.

4

Set your price and earn

You choose your own monthly price. We handle payments through Stripe, manage subscriptions, and automate access. You keep 80% of every payment. Revenue hits your account monthly.

Trust & verification

We don't list anything we haven't verified ourselves. Every strategy goes through this process before it reaches the marketplace.

📊

3+ year backtests

🎲

✅

We don't trust seller-submitted numbers. We independently verify every backtest result before a strategy can be listed.

📈

Live performance tracking

Once listed, we track live performance over time so you can see how each strategy performs in real market conditions.

Code protection

Your code is never exposed

We use TradingView's built-in protection system to keep strategy source code completely private.

How protected indicators work

Seller publishes as "protected" on TradingView. The indicator runs on charts but the source code is hidden and cannot be viewed, copied, or exported.

Buyer's TradingView username is added to the access list. The indicator appears on their chart with full signals — entry, stop, target, filters.

Buyer cancels → access removed. Their username is taken off the list and the indicator stops working immediately.

Updates are pushed automatically. When the seller updates their strategy, every active subscriber gets the new version instantly.
