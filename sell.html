Recommended indicator features

These aren't required, but strategies with these features tend to perform best on the platform because subscribers can follow along without confusion:

✓ Daily status label — show "No Trade Today," "Long Bias," or "Short Bias" on the chart so subscribers know immediately what to expect
✓ TradingView alerts — push notifications when a signal fires, a trade closes, or it's a no-trade day so subscribers don't have to watch the screen
✓ Clear trading schedule — define the exact days and hours subscribers should be at their chart, and when they can walk away
✓ Visual stop/target lines — show stop loss and take profit directly on the chart so execution is obvious
