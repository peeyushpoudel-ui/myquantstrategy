FAQ

How does the free trial work?↓
30 days full access to the strategy indicator, backtest report, and Monte Carlo results. No charge for 30 days. If you don't subscribe, access is removed.
Can I subscribe to multiple strategies?↓
Yes. Each strategy is a separate subscription at whatever price the seller set.
Can I see the strategy code?↓
No. Strategies are protected indicators on TradingView. You see signals on your chart but the code is never visible.
What happens when I cancel?↓
Your TradingView access is removed and the indicator stops working. You can re-subscribe anytime.
Who sets the prices?↓
Each seller sets their own price. We take 20% and handle payments, verification, and access.
Do I need TradingView?↓
Yes. A free TradingView account works. Signals appear automatically once you're granted access.
