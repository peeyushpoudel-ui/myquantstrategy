Frequently asked questions

How does the 30-day free trial work?↓
Pick any strategy and get 30 days of full access — the protected TradingView indicator, backtest report, Monte Carlo simulation, and trading rules. No credit card required. If you don't subscribe before the trial ends, access is simply removed.
Can I subscribe to multiple strategies?↓
Yes. Each strategy is a separate subscription. Subscribe to as many or as few as you want. Each one bills independently at the price the seller has set.
Can I see the strategy code?↓
No — and that's by design. Strategies are published as protected indicators on TradingView. You see the signals on your chart but the source code is never visible. This protects the seller's intellectual property.
What happens when I cancel?↓
Your TradingView username is removed from the strategy's access list and the indicator stops appearing on your chart. You keep any reports or guides you downloaded. You can re-subscribe anytime.
Who sets the prices?↓
Each strategy creator sets their own price. myquantstrategy is a marketplace — we connect buyers with sellers and handle payments, verification, and access management. We take a 20% platform fee from each subscription.
Do I need TradingView?↓
Yes. All strategies run as protected indicators on TradingView. A free TradingView account works. The signals appear automatically once you're granted access.
