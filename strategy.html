About this strategy

The Peeyush ORB Strategy is a fully mechanical, rule-based 15-minute Opening Range Breakout system designed for MNQ (Micro Nasdaq 100) futures. It requires zero discretion — when the signal fires, you take the trade. No analysis, no interpretation, no second-guessing.

Built and backtested over 3 years of real market data, the strategy averages 5-6 trades per month and requires only 15-30 minutes of screen time per day. It was specifically designed to stay within the drawdown limits of funded trading accounts.

Trading rules overview

01Trading daysTuesday through Friday only. Monday is skipped.
02Volatility filterPrevious day's range must meet a minimum threshold or the day is skipped automatically.
03Directional biasToday's open vs yesterday's close determines long-only or short-only bias.
04Trend filterShort trades require price below a daily moving average.
05Opening rangeThe first 15 minutes define the breakout range. A box appears on the chart.
06Entry signalConfirmation candles must close outside the range in the bias direction.
07News filterA blackout window around major economic releases filters out noise.
08Risk managementFixed stop loss and take profit. 1:7 risk-to-reward ratio.
09Position sizingFixed contract size. One trade per day maximum.
10EOD closeAll positions close before market close. No overnight risk.
